About Me

I am originally born & raised in Lawton, Oklahoma. I attended the University of Oklahoma, in Norman, OK, for my undergraduate degree. I obtained a Bachelor of Science in 2010 specalizing in Multidisciplinary Studies: Technical Sciences. In that time, I began to study base elements of Solidworks and Java. This is where my love of technology and software began to grow. After I graduated from the University of Oklahoma, I moved to Oklahoma City, Oklahoma to begin my career. I started in the oil & gas industry as an independent landman. This was very enjoyable, and I found the work very satisfying, as well.

I moved to Denver, Colorado in March of 2013, and instantly fell in love with everything this state has to offer. The most important reason for getting to be here was that I had landed a new job working for Garlock Pipeline Technologies in Wheatridge, Colorado. In my spare time I enjoy spending time with my family and friends, distance running, skiing, riding my bike, experiencing live music, camping, reading, yoga, and traveling.
